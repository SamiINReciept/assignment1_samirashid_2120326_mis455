Molecular Dynamics
As a part of our esteemed team from a leading US university, I am excited to contribute to the creation of a standardized repository for molecular dynamics simulations. Working alongside renowned experts, I am dedicated to ensuring the reliability and accessibility of simulation data, accelerating advancements in the field. Together, we strive to establish a centralized hub that promotes transparency and collaboration among researchers worldwide, driving forward the frontier of molecular dynamics research.
Protein's interaction with other molecular particles is simulated using software packages such as GROMACS or CHARMM gui.
Structural information and other data about the simulation is stored in a metadata file, which is generated using MDrepo.org
