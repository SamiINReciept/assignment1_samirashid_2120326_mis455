Particle Physics
As a member of our dynamic team of student research assistants and esteemed professors, I am thrilled to be engaged in the exploration of particle physics. Our collective focus revolves around unraveling the intricate behaviors of particles, delving into the realms of quantum mechanics at the smallest scales. Our ultimate aim is to forge collaborative ties with CERN, leveraging our insights and expertise to contribute meaningfully to their pioneering projects. Together, we aspire to deepen our understanding of particle physics, paving the way for groundbreaking advancements in scientific exploration.
Applied for CERN Summer Student Programme 2024 to attend CERN in Geneva, getting involved in the day-to-day work of multicultural teams, attend lectures, visit CERN facilities, take part in discussions and workshops with people who are leaders in their fields.
To understand and predict particle accelerator events, we are in the process of getting familiarized with a simulation and visualization tool called Phoenix.
